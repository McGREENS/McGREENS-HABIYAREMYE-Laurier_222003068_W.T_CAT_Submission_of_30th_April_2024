Biography

Laurier Habiyaemye is the visionary leader behind ABS Lines, a leading provider of ambulance services dedicated to ensuring prompt and compassionate care for communities in need. With a passion for leveraging technology to improve healthcare accessibility, Laurier founded ABS Lines while pursuing his Bachelor's degree in Business Information Technology (BIT) at university.

As CEO, Laurier leads ABS Lines with integrity, innovation, and a deep sense of responsibility to the communities it serves. His unwavering dedication to excellence and commitment to delivering high-quality care have earned him recognition as a respected leader in the field.
